1. Fast Track Clinic in FYKH
GEM nurse consults Geriatrician for patient requiring Fast Track Clinic in FYKH.
Contact FYKH GDH nurse for Fast Track Clinic appointment, Tel: 2855 6177.
Fax GaFD referral form (Appendix 18), filled and endorsed by Geriatrician, to GDH. Fax: 2855 6169.
GDH clerk books clinic, confirms with Geriatrician, faxes reply slip to ICDS office Fax: 2986 3192, and contacts GEM nurse.
GEM nurse notifies patient of confirmed appointment with self-arranged transport.
2. Geriatric Day Hospital (FYKH / TWH)
GEM nurse consults Geriatrician for patient requiring GDH in FYKH or TWH.
FYKH GDH Tel: 2855 6177 · TWH GDH Tel: 2589 8178.
Fax GaFD GDH referral form (Appendix 18) endorsed by Geriatrician. FYKH Fax: 2855 6169 · TWH Fax: 2546 5510.
GDH clerk books appointment, faxes reply slip to ICDS office Fax: 2986 3192.
GEM nurse informs patient of confirmed GDH appointment with self-arranged transport.
3. Advancing SOPD Appointment
GEM nurse consults Geriatrician for patient requiring advancing SOPD appointment.
Contact SOPD (QMH / GH / TWH) by fax with referral form (Appendix 18) endorsed by Geriatrician, plus doctor referral letter.
For memory / fall / nutritional clinic in QMH, contact QMH geriatric nurse directly for advancing booking.
Early appointment will be arranged by SOPD, then GEM nurse informs patient by phone.
